VALMAtrack

ValmaTrack is an attendance tracking system powered by RFID technology. When a student scans their ID, the system instantly records attendance, notifies parents, and logs whether the student is on time or late.

This provides an efficient and reliable way to record the exact time of student arrivals. This system promotes security, accountability, and punctuality among students.
